Over the past few weeks, the Cloud Platform Engineering team has been actively engaging with application owners to coordinate TLS upgrades across our Function Apps and Web Apps.

This initiative is part of our ongoing commitment to strengthening the security posture of our platform. By moving to more secure TLS versions, we help ensure that data in transit is protected against modern threats and aligned with current industry standards and compliance requirements.

While these upgrades are largely transparent to end users, they are critical in reducing vulnerabilities associated with older protocols. Our team has been working closely with application owners to assess impact, schedule updates, and provide support where needed to ensure a smooth transition.

If you’ve already collaborated with us on this—thank you for your partnership. If not, you may hear from us soon as we continue this rollout.

As always, our goal is to keep our platform secure, resilient, and future-ready.
